About

The Architect Behind
the Intelligence.

I'm Sanskriti Bhushan — a Full Stack Engineer at CNEAPEE Services, where I operate at the intersection of systems engineering and artificial intelligence. My current mandate is ambitious by design: to architect and ship CNEAPEE OS, a next-generation AI ecosystem that unifies web-based and locally-running language models under a single, coherent runtime.

My engineering vocabulary spans the full vertical stack — from LLM quantization research (squeezing 4-bit precision inference out of models that previously demanded cloud-scale compute) down to the React components and REST APIs users actually touch. I believe the best AI products are the ones where the seams between intelligence and interface are completely invisible.

Before CNEAPEE, I honed my design sensibility as a UI/UX Designer Intern at Unified Consultancy Services, shipping the Texora-AI interface, and deepened my mobile engineering fundamentals as an Android Developer Intern with Google for Developers. Those two poles — rigorous interface craft and systematic engineering discipline — define how I build everything.

I'm completing my B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering at Galgotias University (Class of 2026), concurrently shipping production systems. The classroom and the codebase are the same place for me.
